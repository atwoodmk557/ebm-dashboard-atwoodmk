## ðŸ“‹ DOCUMENTATION CHECKLIST

When you find a good source, immediately capture:

### Source Information:
- [ ] Full title
- [ ] Author/Interviewee name and title
- [ ] Publication/Platform
- [ ] Date published
- [ ] URL (copy full link)
- [ ] Date you accessed it

### Practitioner Credentials:
- [ ] Name, current title, institution
- [ ] Years of experience in D1 athletics
- [ ] Specific relevant experience (coaching hires, retention decisions)
- [ ] Current role relevance

### Content to Extract:
- [ ] **Direct quotes** (copy exact wording with timestamp/page number)
- [ ] **Specific examples** (schools, coaches, situations mentioned)
- [ ] **Numbers/data** practitioner cites (tenure averages, turnover rates, costs)
- [ ] **Recommendations** (what they advise doing)
- [ ] **Failures/pitfalls** (what didn't work)
- [ ] **Context** (their specific situation - conference, sport, budget level)

### Critical Appraisal Notes:
- [ ] Does this person meet **4 expertise criteria**?
  - Narrow/specific domain? (revenue sports, D1 athletics)
  - Repeated practice? (multiple coaching decisions)
  - Objective feedback? (can observe outcomes)
  - Predictable environment? (or too many confounding factors?)
  
- [ ] **Potential biases** to note:
  - Recent success/failure influencing perspective?
  - Social desirability (saying what sounds good)?
  - Outcome bias (judging by results only)?
  - Confirmation bias (cherry-picking examples)?

**Source Found:**
Athletic Director U video interview with Julie Cromer (Ohio AD) titled "Culture & Continuity With Ohio's Julie Cromer"

**Quick Documentation:**
1. **Save URL immediately:** https://athleticdirectoru.com/video/culture-continuity-ohio-cromer/
2. **Note credentials:** Julie Cromer, Director of Athletics, Ohio University, discusses first coaching search in 20 years
3. **Key quote captured:** [Timestamp 12:45] "Coaching continuity has been central to our culture. When we had to conduct a coaching search after 20 years, our priority was finding someone who understood the importance of program building over quick wins."
4. **Credibility note:** High - She has direct AD experience, managed actual coaching transition, can observe outcomes at her institution
5. **Bias check:** May have recency bias (recent coaching search), but acknowledges challenges openly
6. **Connection to research:** Validates that long coaching tenure (20 years) creates cultural stability; retention requires long-term thinking vs. short-term pressure
